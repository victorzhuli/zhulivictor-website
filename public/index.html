Li Zhu

Staff Research Engineer II at Samsung Research America

I am a Staff Research Engineer II at Digital Health Lab, Samsung Research America, Mountain View, CA. I lead AI-driven innovation at the intersection of wearable sensing and real-world health monitoring, delivering solutions from prototyping to clinical validation.

My research has been featured on the IEEE JBHI journal cover (2023), and I have authored 45+ publications at top-tier venues with 15+ patents filed. My work spans cuffless blood pressure monitoring, contactless vital sign detection, atrial fibrillation burden estimation, and advanced bio-sensing technologies.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Rutgers University in 2018, where I developed computational methods for predicting behavior from neuroimaging data. Prior to my Ph.D., I spent nearly a decade in the consumer electronics industry in China, including five years as an R&D Manager leading a 20+ member multidisciplinary team in hardware design and product development for professional audio/video systems.
